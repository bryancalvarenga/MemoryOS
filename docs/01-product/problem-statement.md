# Problem Statement

## Background

Modern knowledge workers accumulate large amounts of information through various digital channels.

Examples include:

- technical documentation
- PDFs
- articles
- bookmarks
- notes
- code repositories

Over time, this information becomes difficult to retrieve.

## Core Problem

Users frequently remember that they encountered a piece of information in the past but cannot recall:

- where it was stored
- when it was accessed
- what exact keywords were used

This results in lost knowledge and repeated research.

## Why Existing Solutions Fail

Most current tools focus on storage rather than retrieval.

Examples include:

- note-taking applications
- bookmarking tools
- document storage systems

While these tools allow users to store information, they do not effectively support semantic retrieval of past knowledge.

## Opportunity

A system capable of indexing personal knowledge and retrieving it through semantic understanding can dramatically improve productivity and knowledge reuse.

MemoryOS aims to address this gap.
